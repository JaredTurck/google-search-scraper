The cat is a domestic species of small carnivorous mammal. It is the only domesticated species in the family Felidae and is often referred to as the domestic cat to distinguish it from the wild members of the family. Wikipedia
Lifespan: 2 – 16 years (In the wild)
Gestation period: 58 – 67 days
Daily sleep: 12 – 16 hours
Mass: 3.6 – 4.5 kg (Adult)
Scientific name: Felis catus
Height: 23 – 25 cm
